Bio

I am a PhD student in Database Research Group @ University of Michigan EECS. I am co-supervised by Prof. Mike Cafarella and Prof. H. V. Jagadish (jag). My research interest lies in data preparation and data integration. I interned in Trifacta in summer 2017 working on format standardization. I received B.S. degrees in computer science and mathematics from Purdue University. Before transfering to Purdue, I studied EE for two years in Tianjin University in China.
