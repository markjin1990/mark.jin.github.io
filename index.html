About Me

I am a PhD student in Database Research Group @ University of Michigan CSE. I am co-supervised by Prof. Mike Cafarella and Prof. H. V. Jagadish (jag). My research interest lies in improving self-service data preparation using a combination of AI, HCI and PL techniques. I interned in Trifacta in summer 2017 working on data pattern normalization mentored by Sean Kandel, Mike Minar, and Prof. Joe Hellerstein. The work was integrated into Trifacta Cloud Wrangler launched in 08/2018. I received B.S. degrees in computer science and mathematics from Purdue University. Before transfering to Purdue, I studied EE for two years in Tianjin University, a top university in engineering in China. Please check my CV for more details.
